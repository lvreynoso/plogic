FONTLOG
Apparatus SIL font family
========================


This file provides detailed information on the Apparatus SIL family of fonts.
This information should be distributed along with the Apparatus SIL fonts and
any derivative works.


Basic Font Information
----------------------

The Apparatus SIL Fonts were designed to provide most of the symbols needed 
to reproduce the textual apparatus found in major editions of Greek & Hebrew 
biblical texts.


Four fonts from this typeface family are included in this release:

     * Apparatus SIL Regular
     * Apparatus SIL Bold
     * Apparatus SIL Italic
     * Apparatus SIL Bold Italic
